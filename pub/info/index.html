The Ceres Project

What it the Ceres Project?

The Ceres Project is a special type of modular plant pot. It is built for the ISS, so it has to work in space. The original design was an octogon, but we changed it to a hexagon as it fits together just as easily and it requires less construction. We are plannng on planting chia seeds, potatoes, lentils and tomatoes. In a nutshell, flat pack plants for space.
